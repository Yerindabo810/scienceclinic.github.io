About Science Clinic Uganda

Empowering Science Education Since 2015

Science Clinic Uganda is a non-profit organization dedicated to advancing STEM education through innovative programs, teacher training, and student mentorship.

Our Mission

To provide quality science education resources and training to students and educators across Uganda.

Our Vision

A Uganda where every student has access to excellent science education and practical learning opportunities.

Our Programs

Student Bootcamps

Intensive practical science training for secondary school students.

Teacher Training

Professional development workshops for science educators.

Lab Development

Equipping schools with modern science laboratory facilities.
